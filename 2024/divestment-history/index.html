Methodology

This project focused on pro-divestment activism and the administration's response. Student- or faculty-led counter-activism events were not included due to project scope. A pro-divestment activism event was defined as follows:

  • All articles from the Maroon website or digitized archives which:
    • Contained the word "divest" from search results
    • Had divestment as the main subject, excluding personality-based attacks (some letters to the editor were not focused on arguing divestment as a moral or political issue)
    • Could be defined as a discrete event (i.e., not a general explainer or background for divestment movements)
    • Were located on UChicago campus or involved UChicago students (as opposed to reporting about other schools or Chicago city politics)
